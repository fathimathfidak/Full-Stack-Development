Upcoming Events

1.National Hackathon 2025

A 48-hour coding challenge bringing together developers, designers, and innovators from across India. Participants will work in teams to build creative solutions around themes like smart cities, healthcare, fintech, and cybersecurity. Winning teams receive prizes, internships, and startup funding opportunities.

2.World Mental Health Day Seminar

In observance of World Mental Health Day, this event features expert talks, panel discussions, and Q&A sessions addressing anxiety, depression, and youth mental health. Open to students, professionals, and the public. Free registration includes access to downloadable resources and wellness toolkits.

Past Events

1.International Women's Day Celebration 2025

This event celebrated the achievements of women in education, technology, and entrepreneurship. It included motivational talks by women leaders, panel discussions, and cultural performances. A special felicitation ceremony was held to honor outstanding female students and faculty members.

2.National Science Day Expo 2025

Held to commemorate the discovery of the Raman Effect by Sir C.V. Raman, the expo featured interactive science exhibits, student project showcases, and hands-on workshops for school and college students. Scientists from ISRO and BARC delivered guest lec tures to promote scientific thinking.
